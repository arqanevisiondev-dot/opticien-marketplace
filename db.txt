Use TCP (not socket) for Prisma and create/seed
Point Prisma to TCP on 127.0.0.1
export DATABASE_URL="mysql://root:@127.0.0.1:3306/opticien_marketplace"
Generate, create tables, and seed
pnpm prisma generate
pnpm prisma db push
pnpm db:seed
pnpm tsx prisma/seed-categories.ts
pnpm tsx prisma/seed-options.ts
